## Name Game
This one requires us to go through a PCAP file, with a lot of it just being DNS requests and responses. After looking through `Statistics` and a little bit of their contents, I don't find anything so I resort to finding `ctf` in the packet list, bytes, and details and I see that certain DNS requests are for weird-looking domain names. These domains have `totallynotac2` in it so they are already looking suspicious. 

They don't have much in their packet bytes either, but I do notice that their subdomain names are these hexadecimal-looking strings. Therefore, for each of these packets, I get that subdomain name and parse it in CyberChef to go from hexadecimal to ASCII. This gets me the flag. 

This looks like one of those DNS exfiltration.
